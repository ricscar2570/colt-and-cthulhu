  • Introduce setting, NPCs, normalit√†
  • Party completa jobs mundane (bounty hunting, escort, protezione)
  • Gradualmente introduce elementi weird (rumors, strani eventi)
  • Primo horror encounter (zombi, cultisti minori)
  • Climax Act I: Scoperta cospirazione larger (cultisti non random, piano orchestrato)

  • Party investigates cospirazione attivamente
  • Horror encounters pi√π frequenti, dangerous (Deep Ones, Mi-Go, vampiri)

  • Multiple challenges simultaneously (combat cultisti + prevent ritual + rescue hostage + survive horror)

  • Resolution: Victory (seal Great Old One, banish), Failure (apocalypse, TPK), Pyrrhic (win but half party dead/insane)

Pacing

Regola 3:1 Ratio

Per ogni 3 sessioni "up" (action, combat, success) ‚Üí 1 sessione "down" (investigation, social, character development, downtime).

Continuous action = player burnout. Continuous roleplay = players antsy per action. Balance!

Tension Curve

Visualizza campaign come wave:

  • Crest (High tension): Combat intenso, horror encounters, chases, time pressure
  • Trough (Low tension): Safe rest in town, shopping, character talks, light investigation
  • Rise: Building toward next crest (clues accumulate, rumors spreading, omens appear)

Alternate crest/trough ogni 2-3 sessioni. Mega-crest (boss fight) ogni 5-8 sessioni.

Foreshadowing

  2. Evidence: "Trovi claw marks su walls miniera. Too large essere bear."
  3. Sound: "Deep in tunnels, senti... slithering. Qualcosa massive moving."

  • Ambient sound: Thunderstorm loop durante cemetery exploration. Silence improvviso when horror appears.
  • Lighting: Dim lights tavolo. Candlelight durante horror scenes. Flashlight quando party explores dungeon (point light solo limited area visible).
  • Music cues: Tense strings quando investigating. Silence quando hiding from monster. Crescendo quando chase/combat.
