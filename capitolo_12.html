12.4 Mastering Horror Efficacemente

Tipi di Orrore

Colt & Cthulhu usa 3 tipi horror. Varia per keep fresh:

  • Descrizioni viscerali: sangue, mutilazioni, morte violenta
  • Esempio: "Corpo Mary-Ann aperto gola a sternum. Ribs cracked open come cage. Organs mancanti."
  • Impatto: Shock immediato, disgusto
  • Uso: Sparingly. Overuse = desensitization.

2. Horror Psicologico (Madness)

  • Mind-bending concepts, realt√† questioned, sanity degrading

12.7 Gestire Problemi Tavolo

Problem Player Types & Solutions

1. The Rules Lawyer

Sintomi: Constantly challenges rulings, cita rules obscuri, debate meccaniche mid-session.

Sintomi: Domina conversazioni, interrupts altri, sempre center attention.

Soluzione:

  • Private talk: "Noto parli molto. Great enthusiasm! Ma assicuriamoci everyone has chance contribute. Ok?"
  • Structured turns: "Going around table clockwise per input. Jake primo, then Sarah, etc." Enforces equality.

3. The Passive Player

Sintomi: Rarely speaks, goes with flow, disengaged.

Soluzione:

  • Direct questions: "Mike, cosa fa Ranger questo momento?"

  • Character hook: "Mike, Ranger nota tracks familiari [tie backstory]. What do?" Engage personal investment.
  • Se genuinamente disinterested: "Ok lasciare anytime. No hard feelings." Non force.

Sintomi: Kills every NPC, loots everything, no regard consequences OR morality.

Soluzione:

  • Consequences: "Uccidi merchant. Guardie witness. Wanted poster $500 bounty. Marshal + posse hunt you." Actions matter.
  • Session zero: "This campaign emphasizes story, choices, morality. Murder hobo style not fit. Everyone agree?"
  • Redemption arc: "Character uccide tanti. Inizia haunted victims ghosts. Must atone O go insane." Force character growth.
  • Last resort: "Playstyle not compatible campaign. Maybe find different group?"

5. The Phone Addict

Sintomi: Constantly checking phone, distracted, miss cues.

Soluzione:

  • Table rule: "Phones away durante session salvo emergency. Break ogni 2 ore check texts." Clear expectation.
  • Gentle reminder: "Hey, Mike, tuo turno. Attenzione?" Publicly friendly nudge.
  • Private talk: "Noto phone distraction. Important stuff? If not enjoying, ok say so." Check interest level.
